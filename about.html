About me

Dr. Travis Bradberry

World's bestselling emotional intelligence author · LinkedIn Top Voice

Award-winning author of the #1 bestselling book, The New Emotional Intelligence, helping millions of people around the world raise their EQ.

Dr. Travis Bradberry is the award-winning author of the #1 bestselling book, The New Emotional Intelligence.

As the world's bestselling emotional intelligence author, his work has sold over 5 million books and is available in more than 150 countries. He applies the latest breakthroughs in psychology and neuroscience to help people everywhere develop their emotional intelligence.

Dr. Bradberry is a LinkedIn Top Voice with 2.6 million followers, and his work has been covered by TIME, The Wall Street Journal, USA Today, Bloomberg BusinessWeek, Fortune, Forbes, Fast Company, The Washington Post, and The Harvard Business Review.

He has spoken to and worked with organizations including AT&T, Salesforce, Microsoft, Intel, Coca-Cola, and Wells Fargo, as well as at The Global Leadership Summit, The Fortune Growth Summit, and The Conference Board: Learning from Legends.
